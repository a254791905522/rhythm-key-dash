Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data Collection

Rhythm Key Dash is a completely offline single-player game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No user accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. There are no features that require or request any personal data from users of any age.

Data Security

All game progress is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS device-level security. No data is ever transmitted to any server. There is no cloud storage, no syncing, and no external data access.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics tools, or social media services. There are no external connections made by this application.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us through our App Store page.
